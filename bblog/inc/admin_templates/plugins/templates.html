Here is where you can change the look of your blog, by editing the templates. Your site is divided into 6 templates;

  • css - you put your css here
  • header - the global header on all pages
  • footer - the global footer on all pages
  • index - your blog's homepage
  • post - the 'view post page' which displays a single post, along with comments and trackbacks
  • archives - displays a list of all your posts
